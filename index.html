A note to our fans,

Thank you for the overwhelming support you've given us this past year. It's incredible to see you share our excitement for Napa Acoustic's upcoming product lines. Many of you have been wondering what's come of us since we were bestowed Most Innovative Design at the California Audio Show last year. Rest assured that we've been hard at work designing and perfecting brand new products that we know you will love.

Here are just a few things that have changed from a year ago:

  • Moved to a larger office (thanks to you!)
  • Brought manufacuring closer to home (because America)
  • Questioned how we could deliver unprecedented quality (only the best for our fans)
  • Answered how we could deliver unprecedented quality (hint: handcraft)

Then of course there are things that will never change. Like our mission to make amazing audiophile products affordable for everyone. Take a peek at some of the things we've been working on and catch us for our product line debut at California Audio Show '18 in June. We hope you are as excited as we are!

Best,
Joseph Kwong
President | Napa Acoustic
